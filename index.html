Billions of people use Whatsapp. And there are billions of community groups. A group sustains and provides value when there is active engagement from the members. We need to understand the participation statistics.

WhatsApp Posts Analyzer (WAPS)is created to help with such quick and easy analysis.

The v1 Whatsapp Post Analyzer analyzes the chat conversations with privacy. You can load the whatsapp chat conversations and quickly receive information on posters, posting frequency, and the sessions.
